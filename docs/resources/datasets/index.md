# Datasets

> Public neuroimaging and BCI datasets used for training and benchmarking visual brain decoding models.

| Dataset | Modality | Subjects | Stimuli | Used In |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **[NSD](nsd.md)** | fMRI (7T) | 8 | 73,000 natural images (MS-COCO) | Gaziv 2022, Ozcelik 2023, Huo 2024, Beliy 2026 |
| **[Vim-1](vim-1.md)** | fMRI | 2 | 1,750 natural images | Kay 2008 |
| **[GOD](god.md)** | fMRI | 5 | 1,250 object images (ImageNet) | Horikawa 2017, Huo 2024 |
| **[Horikawa Dream Dataset](god.md#dream-dataset)** | fMRI | 3 | Dream/imagery stimuli | Horikawa 2013, Miliotou 2023 |
| **[ds001506](ds001506.md)** | fMRI (7T) | 3 | 50 natural images + 40 artificial | Shen 2019 |
| **[THINGS](things.md)** | fMRI / EEG / MEG | 3–50 | 22,248 object images (1,854 concepts) | Benchetrit 2023 |
| **[BOLD5000](bold5000.md)** | fMRI (3T) | 4 | 5,254 images (COCO + ImageNet + SUN) | General benchmarking |
| **[EEG-ImageNet](eeg-imagenet.md)** | EEG | 6 | 2,000 images (40 ImageNet classes) | General benchmarking |
| **[LoongX](loongx.md)** | EEG / fNIRS / PPG | 12 | ~24,000 image-edit pairs | Zhou 2025 |
