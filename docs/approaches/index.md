# Approaches

> Abstract decoding paradigms and alignment methodologies used in vision-brain modeling.

Rather than looking at individual downstream tasks, computational strategies in this field map brain activity to visual content through several generalized paradigms:

- **[Direct Mapping](direct-mapping.md)**: Supervised translation of brain signals to pixels or pre-trained neural network activations.
- **[Latent Alignment](latent-alignment.md)**: Projecting brain representations into a shared, pre-aligned semantic space (such as CLIP).
- **[Generative Conditioning](generative-conditioning.md)**: Injecting decoded brain embeddings to guide pre-trained generative networks (VAEs, Diffusion, DiT).
- **[Interactive Feedback](interactive-feedback.md)**: Operating closed-loop BCI systems that optimize or edit visual content based on real-time brain states.
